# SkillScope AI – Job Market Analytics & Career Intelligence Platform

SkillScope AI is an enterprise-grade analytics platform designed to help students, job seekers, and career changers understand technical skill demand, salary metrics, monthly hiring timelines, and regional location densities. The platform utilizes **Pandas & NumPy** to ingest and process a generated database of **50,000+ job records** and features **Google Gemini AI integrations** to offer automated resume scanner evaluations and customized three-month learning roadmaps.


### Prerequisites
Make sure you have the following installed on your system:
1. **Python 3.8+** (pip included)
2. **Node.js 18+** (npm included)


### Setup Steps
1. Open a terminal and navigate to the project directory:
   ```bash
   cd c:/Users/.....
   ```
2. Run the auto-launcher script:
   ```bash
   python start_all.py
   ```


## 📖 User Guide

### 1. Registration & Profiling
- Upon accessing the application, navigate to the **Register** screen.
- Select your target career role (e.g. Data Analyst, AI Engineer, Full Stack Developer).
- Input your current technical stack as a comma-separated list.
- Click register to instantiate your profile and automatically seed dashboard parameters.

### 2. Analytical Dashboards
- **Overview**: Evaluate core metrics (jobs, salaries, demand tags) and toggle the role filter to observe how market specifications pivot between different careers.
- **Skills Analyzer**: Track the top 20 requested technologies, class distributions, time trends, and observe emerging categories.
- **Salary Analytics**: View base distribution histograms, check average salary benchmarks, and examine experience level modifiers.
- **Location Intelligence**: Hover over states in the interactive USA map to see active openings, view top-hiring cities, and remote accessibility factors.

### 3. Actionable Career Advisors
- **Skill Gap Matrix**: Select a desired role to compare your capabilities against top market demand skills, obtaining a readiness score and list of missing technologies.
- **Resume Scanner**: Drag and drop your resume PDF or text file. The parser matches keywords to check alignment, while the AI Recruiter offers specific formatting and content edits.
- **AI Career Advisor**: View your personalized 3-month project roadmap, primary learning targets, and job search strategies tailored by Gemini AI.
- **Reports Center**: Export your complete profile, gap analysis, roadmap, and resume reviews into a downloadable PDF dossier with a single click.
